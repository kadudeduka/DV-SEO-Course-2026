### **5. Guided Lab 1**
- **Lab Title:** Exploring the Real Search Landscape  
- **Objective:** By completing this lab, learners will systematically analyze multiple Google SERPs to identify and document organic listings, paid ads, SERP features, and zero-click elements, demonstrating understanding of where SEO visibility exists in the modern search ecosystem and how different query types produce different SERP layouts.
- **Brief Description:**  
  Participants perform multiple Google searches for different query types (informational, commercial, navigational, transactional) and systematically analyze the search results pages. They identify and document organic listings, paid ads, featured snippets, People Also Ask sections, image results, video carousels, local packs, and other SERP features. Through this hands-on observation, learners understand where SEO visibility truly exists today and how search engines signal intent through SERP layout. This lab reinforces understanding of the modern search ecosystem and prepares learners for intent validation in later chapters.
- **Tools Used:** Google Search (free, accessible to all learners)
- **Submission Format:** [Reference: Day_1_Lab_1_Submission_Format.md]

---

### **6. Guided Lab 2**
- **Lab Title:** SEO Terminology in Action  
- **Objective:** By completing this lab, learners will map common SEO terminology (crawl, index, rank, intent, SERP, impressions, CTR, authority) to real examples using Google Search and Google Search Central documentation, demonstrating ability to explain SEO concepts clearly and use professional terminology accurately.
- **Brief Description:**  
  Learners systematically map key SEO terms introduced in Day 1 chapters to real examples they can observe and reference. Using Google Search to find real instances and Google Search Central for official definitions, participants create a terminology reference document that connects concepts to practical examples. This hands-on mapping builds confidence with SEO language and prepares learners to explain SEO concepts clearly to stakeholders, reinforcing the Day 1 learning outcome of clearly explaining SEO to non-technical audiences.
- **Tools Used:** Google Search, Google Search Central (developers.google.com/search) - both free and accessible to all learners
- **Submission Format:** [Reference: Day_1_Lab_2_Submission_Format.md]
