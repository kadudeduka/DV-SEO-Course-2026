### Step 2: Assess Programmatic SEO Appropriateness

**What to Do:**
Assess whether programmatic SEO is appropriate:

1. **Evaluate appropriateness criteria:**
   - **Scale:** Does the scenario require scalable content creation?
   - **Data availability:** Is there unique data or information available?
   - **Template potential:** Can content be created from templates with unique data?
   - **Value provision:** Can programmatic content provide unique value?

2. **Assess appropriateness:**
   - **Appropriate:** Large scale, unique data, template potential, unique value
   - **Potentially appropriate:** Moderate scale, some unique data, template potential
   - **Not appropriate:** Small scale, no unique data, no template potential, no unique value

3. **Document appropriateness assessment:**
   - Record appropriateness evaluation
   - Note criteria assessment
   - Document appropriateness determination
   - Create appropriateness summary

**Tool to Use:**
Documentation Tool, Day 19 Chapter 1

**What to Record:**

- Appropriateness criteria evaluation
- Appropriateness determination (appropriate, potentially appropriate, not appropriate)
- Appropriateness reasoning
- Appropriateness summary

**Checkpoint:**

- [ ] You have assessed programmatic SEO appropriateness

- [ ] You have recorded appropriateness assessment in the Submission Template

### Step 3: Evaluate Ethical Considerations

**What to Do:**
Evaluate ethical considerations for programmatic SEO:

1. **Assess ethical criteria:**
   - **Human oversight:** Will programmatic content have human review?
   - **Unique value:** Does programmatic content provide unique value?
   - **Guideline compliance:** Does programmatic approach comply with guidelines?
   - **Quality standards:** Can programmatic content meet quality standards?

2. **Evaluate ethics:**
   - **Ethical:** Human oversight, unique value, guideline compliance, quality standards
   - **Potentially ethical:** Some oversight, some unique value, mostly compliant
   - **Unethical:** No oversight, no unique value, guideline violations, low quality

### Step 4: Assess Scalability

**What to Do:**
Assess scalability of programmatic SEO approach:

1. **Evaluate scalability factors:**
   - **Content volume:** How many pages/content pieces can be created?
   - **Data availability:** Is there enough unique data for scale?
   - **Template efficiency:** Can templates create content efficiently?
   - **Maintenance:** Can programmatic content be maintained at scale?

2. **Assess scalability:**
   - **Highly scalable:** Large volume potential, sufficient data, efficient templates
   - **Moderately scalable:** Moderate volume, some data, workable templates
   - **Not scalable:** Limited volume, insufficient data, inefficient templates
