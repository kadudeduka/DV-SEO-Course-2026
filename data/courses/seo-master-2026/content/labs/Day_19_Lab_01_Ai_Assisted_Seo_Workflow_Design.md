**Day:** Day 19 — AI-Assisted SEO, Automation & Programmatic SEO  
**Lab Number:** Lab 1

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: I don't understand AI tool concepts

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about AI tool capabilities.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 19 Chapter 1 for AI tool guidance
2. AI assists: Research, content creation, analysis
3. AI doesn't replace: Human judgment, quality control, strategic thinking
4. Focus on understanding AI assistance conceptually
5. When in doubt, document your understanding

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the AI-assisted workflow framework conceptually. The learning is in the workflow design process, not perfect AI tool knowledge.

---

### Issue: I'm not sure where AI can assist

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about AI integration opportunities.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 19 Chapter 1 for AI assistance guidance
2. **Research:** AI can analyze data, identify patterns
3. **Content:** AI can generate drafts, suggest improvements
4. **Analysis:** AI can process data, generate reports
5. When in doubt, document your reasoning

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the AI integration framework conceptually. The learning is in the workflow design process, not perfect AI integration identification.

---

### Issue: I don't understand human oversight needs

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about where human oversight is needed.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 19 Chapter 1 for oversight guidance
2. Human oversight needed: Quality control, accuracy verification, strategic decisions
3. AI assists, but humans ensure quality and strategy
4. Design oversight at critical points
5. When in doubt, document your reasoning

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the oversight framework conceptually. The learning is in the workflow design process, not perfect oversight design.
