## Lab Steps

### Step 1: Analyze Corporate Website and Business Goals

**What to Do:**
Analyze the corporate website and identify business goals:

1. **Review corporate website:**
   - Access provided corporate website example
   - Review website structure and content
   - Identify website type (service provider, SaaS, professional services, etc.)
   - Note website characteristics

2. **Identify business goals:**
   - Lead generation (primary goal for most corporate sites)
   - Brand visibility (secondary goal)
   - Customer acquisition
   - Revenue generation
   - Other business objectives

3. **Document analysis:**
   - Record website type and characteristics
   - List business goals identified
   - Note primary vs secondary goals

### Step 2: Define SEO Goals Aligned with Business Objectives

**What to Do:**
Define SEO goals that support business goals:

1. **Align SEO with business goals:**
   - Lead generation → SEO goals: Rankings for lead-generating keywords, service page optimization
   - Brand visibility → SEO goals: Brand keyword rankings, brand mention visibility
   - Customer acquisition → SEO goals: Commercial-intent keyword rankings, conversion optimization

2. **Define specific SEO goals:**
   - Target keywords (lead-generating, commercial-intent)
   - Page types to optimize (service pages, product pages)
   - Ranking objectives
   - Traffic objectives (qualified traffic, not just volume)

3. **Document SEO goals:**
   - Record SEO goals defined

   - Document goal prioritization
   - Create SEO goal summary

### Step 3: Select Appropriate SEO Tactics

**What to Do:**
Select SEO tactics appropriate for corporate websites:

1. **Service/Product page optimization:**
   - Optimize service pages for lead-generating keywords
   - Optimize product pages for commercial-intent keywords
   - Focus on conversion optimization
   - Ensure pages generate leads effectively

2. **Limited, high-quality content:**
   - Create focused content (service guides, case studies, resources)
   - Avoid extensive blog libraries (content site approach)
   - Focus on quality over quantity
   - Support lead generation and brand authority

3. **Local SEO (if applicable):**
   - Optimize for local search
   - Claim and optimize Google Business Profile
   - Build local citations
   - Focus on local lead generation

4. **Document tactic selection:**
   - Record tactics selected
   - Note why tactics are appropriate for corporate sites
   - Document tactic prioritization
   - Create tactic selection summary

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: I can't identify business goals

**Symptoms:**
You're having trouble identifying business goals for the corporate website.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 17 Chapter 1 for business goal guidance
2. Corporate sites typically prioritize: Lead generation, brand visibility
3. Consider website type (service provider, SaaS, etc.)
4. Look for conversion goals, lead forms, contact pages
5. When in doubt, document your reasoning

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the business goal identification framework conceptually. The learning is in the strategy design process, not perfect goal identification.

---

### Issue: I'm not sure how to align SEO goals with business goals

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about SEO-business alignment.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 17 Chapter 1 for alignment guidance
2. Lead generation business goal → SEO goals: Rankings for lead-generating keywords
3. Brand visibility business goal → SEO goals: Brand keyword rankings
4. Ensure SEO goals support business objectives, not exist independently
5. When in doubt, document your reasoning

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the alignment framework conceptually. The learning is in the strategy design process, not perfect alignment.

---

### Issue: I don't understand corporate vs content site differences

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about corporate-specific tactics and KPIs.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 17 Chapter 1 for corporate vs content differences
2. **Corporate sites:** Limited pages, lead generation focus, service page optimization
3. **Content sites:** Extensive content, traffic growth focus, blog optimization
4. Corporate KPIs focus on leads, not just traffic volume
5. When in doubt, document your understanding

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the corporate SEO framework conceptually. The learning is in the strategy design process, not perfect tactic selection.
