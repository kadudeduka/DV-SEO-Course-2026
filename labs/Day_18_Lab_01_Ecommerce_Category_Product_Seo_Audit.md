### Step 2: Audit Category Page Optimization

**What to Do:**
Audit category page optimization:

1. **Check category page elements:**
   - Title tags (optimized for broader commercial keywords?)
   - Meta descriptions (present, relevant?)
   - Headings (H1 present, relevant?)
   - Content (category overview, product showcases?)

2. **Assess category optimization:**
   - Are category pages optimized for broader commercial queries?
   - Do category pages showcase products effectively?
   - Is filtering/navigation optimized?
   - Are category pages structured for SEO?

3. **Identify category issues:**
   - Missing or weak optimization elements
   - Poor keyword targeting
   - Weak content or structure
   - Other category-specific issues

4. **Document category audit:**
   - Record category page audit findings
   - List optimization issues identified
   - Note category-specific gaps
   - Assess category optimization quality

**Tool to Use:**
Browser (Inspect Pages), Browser Developer Tools, Documentation Tool

**What to Record:**
Record category audit in the Submission Template, Section 2, Evidence Item 1:
- Category page audit findings
- Optimization issues identified
- Category-specific gaps
- Category optimization quality rating

**Checkpoint:**
You've completed this step when:
- [ ] You have audited category page optimization
- [ ] You have identified category issues
- [ ] You have recorded category audit in the Submission Template

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: I can't distinguish category vs product optimization

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about category vs product page optimization differences.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 18 Chapter 1 for category vs product guidance
2. **Category pages:** Optimize for broader commercial keywords, showcase products
3. **Product pages:** Optimize for specific product keywords, unique descriptions
4. Category = broader queries, Product = specific queries
5. When in doubt, document your understanding

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the e-commerce optimization framework conceptually. The learning is in the audit process, not perfect optimization identification.

---

### Issue: I can't identify faceted navigation issues

**Symptoms:**
You're having trouble identifying faceted navigation SEO challenges.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 18 Chapter 1 for faceted navigation guidance
2. Look for filtering options (color, size, price)
3. Check if filters create multiple URLs
4. Look for canonical tags on filtered pages
5. When in doubt, document your observations

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the faceted navigation framework conceptually. The learning is in the audit process, not perfect issue identification.

---

### Issue: I don't understand e-commerce duplication

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about e-commerce duplication issues.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 18 Chapter 1 for duplication guidance
2. Check if product descriptions are duplicate (manufacturer descriptions)
3. Check if similar products have duplicate content
4. Look for thin product content
5. When in doubt, document your observations

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the duplication framework conceptually. The learning is in the audit process, not perfect duplication identification.
