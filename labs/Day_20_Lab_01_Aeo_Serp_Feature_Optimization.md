**What to Do:**
Apply AEO optimization principles to content:

1. **Optimize for direct answers:**

2. **Optimize content structure:**
   - Use clear headings (H1, H2, H3)

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: I don't understand AEO optimization

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about AEO optimization principles.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 20 Chapter 1 for AEO guidance
2. AEO: Optimize content for direct answers, featured snippets, AI answers
3. Structure content for answer extraction (lists, tables, clear answers)
4. Focus on clarity, conciseness, and direct answers
5. When in doubt, document your understanding

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the AEO framework conceptually. The learning is in the optimization process, not perfect AEO implementation.

---

### Issue: I can't optimize for featured snippets

**Symptoms:**
You're having trouble optimizing for featured snippets.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 20 Chapter 1 for featured snippet guidance
2. Provide clear, concise answers to questions
3. Use lists or tables for structured answers
4. Place key answers near the beginning
5. When in doubt, document your approach

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the featured snippet framework conceptually. The learning is in the optimization process, not perfect snippet optimization.

---

### Issue: I don't understand SERP feature optimization

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about different SERP feature optimization approaches.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 20 Chapter 1 for SERP feature guidance
2. **Featured snippets:** Clear, concise answers, lists/tables
3. **People Also Ask:** Question-answer format, related questions
4. **AI answers:** Clear, authoritative information, structured data
5. When in doubt, document your understanding

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the SERP feature framework conceptually. The learning is in the optimization process, not perfect feature optimization.
