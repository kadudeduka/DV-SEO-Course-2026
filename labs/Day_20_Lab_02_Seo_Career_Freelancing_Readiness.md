## Lab Steps

### Step 1: Inventory SEO Skills Learned

**What to Do:**
Inventory SEO skills learned throughout the course:

1. **Review course content:**
   - Review all days and chapters completed
   - Review all labs completed
   - Identify skills learned and practiced
   - Note skill proficiency levels

2. **Categorize skills:**
   - **Technical SEO skills:** Crawlability, indexation, technical audits
   - **On-page SEO skills:** Optimization, content structure, on-page audits
   - **Content SEO skills:** Keyword research, content strategy, content optimization
   - **Authority SEO skills:** Link building, E-E-A-T, trust signals
   - **Analytical skills:** Auditing, troubleshooting, reporting
   - **Strategic skills:** Strategy design, planning, stakeholder communication

3. **Document skill inventory:**
   - Record all skills learned
   - Note skill categories
   - Document proficiency levels
   - Create skill inventory summary

Documentation Tool, Course Materials

**What to Record:**
Record skill inventory in the Submission Template, Section 1:
- SEO skills learned (comprehensive list)

- Proficiency levels

You've completed this step when:

### Step 4: Assess Career Readiness

**What to Do:**
Assess your career readiness:

1. **Evaluate readiness factors:**
   - **Skill readiness:** Do you have required skills?
   - **Experience readiness:** Do you have hands-on experience?
   - **Portfolio readiness:** Do you have work samples?

2. **Assess readiness level:**
   - **Ready:** Strong skills, experience, portfolio, communication

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: I can't identify all my skills

**Symptoms:**
You're having trouble inventorying all skills learned.

**Solution:**
1. Review course days and chapters systematically
2. Review all labs completed
3. Think about hands-on experience gained
4. Consider both technical and strategic skills
5. When in doubt, document what you know

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the skill inventory framework conceptually. The learning is in the career planning process, not perfect skill identification.

---

### Issue: I don't understand SEO job roles

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about different SEO job roles.

**Solution:**
1. Review career resources provided
2. **SEO Specialist:** General SEO work, audits, optimization
3. **SEO Manager:** Strategy, management, communication
4. **Content SEO:** Content-focused SEO work
5. **Technical SEO:** Technical-focused SEO work
6. When in doubt, document your understanding

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the role mapping framework conceptually. The learning is in the career planning process, not perfect role knowledge.

---

### Issue: I'm not sure how to create a career roadmap

**Symptoms:**
You're uncertain about career roadmap creation.

**Solution:**
1. Review Day 20 Chapter 1 for career guidance
2. Define short-term, medium-term, and long-term goals
3. Plan skill development, experience building, portfolio building
4. Set milestones and checkpoints
5. When in doubt, document your approach

**If This Doesn't Work:**
Focus on understanding the roadmap framework conceptually. The learning is in the career planning process, not perfect roadmap creation.
